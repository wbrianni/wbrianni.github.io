Strawberry Milk

more a drink than a food but still a favorite of mine

Ingredients

  • washed strawberries
  • sugar
  • milk

Directions

  1. mash some strawberries
  2. simmer them in a pot with sugar until it turns into a jam/syrup. Stir when needed
  3. let the jam cool
  4. pour the jam, some chopped strawberries, and milk in a bottle and shake it a little to combine everything
  5. drink it!
